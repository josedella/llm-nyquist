Please connect power adapter, network, PC, handset, and headset 
to the corresponding ports as described in below picture. The

➂ Network port: Connect to LAN that has access to Nyquist server.
➃ PC port: connect PC (optional).
➄ Headset port: connect headset.
➅ Handset port: connect IP Phone handset.
